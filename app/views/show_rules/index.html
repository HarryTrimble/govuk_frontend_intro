Before applying, check the rules for when being a childminder

You must:

  • pay an annual registration fee
  • pay an annual criminal check fee
  • keep records of children under your care
  • report accidents and incidents
  • report changes to premises, contact, staff and childcare hours

You can't:

  • live in the same place as someone else who is disqualified as a childminder
  • If you'll be looking after more than five children, it's required to be accompanied by another registered childminder
